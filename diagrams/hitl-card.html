What this card is doing (design notes)

  • Everything is pre-staged. Classification, context, signals, and a ready draft — the human contribution is one click, not a writing task. The draft is simultaneously parked in the sequencer inbox, so the rep can act from either surface.
  • The confidence line shows its work. 0.62 with the escalation reason — the rep learns to trust the gate because they can see why it fired.
  • The re-route selector is the training-data collector. Approve = confirmation, Edit = style correction, re-route = classification correction. All three land in the decisions ledger and become retrieved few-shot examples for next week's classifier.
  • The draft obeys the language guardrails — correct Czech vocative ("pane Svobodo"), verb forms matching the sender's gender ("jsem se ozvala" — Klára), roles declined to the case the sentence demands, no em-dashes.
